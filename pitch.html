Build Your Pitch!
An elevator pitch is a SHORT speech that makes people excited about your business!
💡Teacher Tip: The hook should be attention-grabbing — a question works great for young kids! Aim for 30-90 seconds total when spoken.
📝 Sample Pitch Opening:
"Have you ever been bored on a rainy day at recess? My name is [Name] and I created [Business] to fix that!"

Practice Your Pitch!
Pick a style, read it out loud, and use the timer to hit 30-90 seconds!
